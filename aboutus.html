A Glimpse

The Department

With the inspiration of Late Mahamana Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya and with the untiring zeal and enthusiasm of Late Professor M.L.Schroff (Father of Pharmacy Education in India), this Department was established as early as in 1932. This is the first Institution in the country to start the Degree level Pharmacy Education and can claim to be the pioneering center for Pharmacy Education in the Asian, African and Far-East region of the world. Students graduated from this Department have spread over US, Europe, UK, Malaysia and other countries.

Spirit '16

A tribute to the majestic river ever enchanting yet evoking within the thought to strive, the Pharmaceutical Society, Department of Pharmaceutics, IIT (BHU), organises for the 7th consecutive year one of our nation's largest pharmaceutical festivals - SPIRIT'16: Experience the Knowledge.The two day event, from 27th-28th of Februrary, promises to emcompass new attributes and innovations to bring out the best of the participants, exposing the technological wing to reveal the magic within. So steady the sails and drive into divinity, an everlasting journey to the world of wits and spiritualism.

National Seminar

SPIRIT'16 packs a unanimous yet thought-evoking conquest in the form of a national platform where the professionals from different streams join hands and share their fair share of knowledge, providing a backdrop of the assiduous lot to put the thought to work.
