Kashi Utkarsh

Kashi Utkarsh is a non-profit Students Organization aimed at the under-privileged kids, promoting literacy and giving them opportunity to take up the big stage, to show case their talents and aspirations. Team SPIRIT is proud to host the health camp for these Special Children under the Kashi Utkarsh Program.
